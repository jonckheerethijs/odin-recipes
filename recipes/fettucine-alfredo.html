Fettucine Alfredo

Description

Ingredients

  • Fettucine pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Romano cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

How to make homemade Fettucine Alfredo step by step

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese
  3. Toss the pasta in the chesse sauce
