Pizza Bianca with Prosciutto

Description

Ingredients

  • Prepared pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella
  • Prosciutto
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Preheat gas grill
  2. Shape pizza dough
  3. Carefully place dough into the grill
  4. Spread ricotta over dough
  5. Sprinkle with salt and italian seasoning
  6. Place cheese on top
  7. Add prosciutto evenly over cheese
  8. Return pizza to the grill over indirect heat
  9. Broil pizza until cheese is golden
